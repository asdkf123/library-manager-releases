Getting Started

1

Add Your Server

Go to Settings ‚Üí Network ‚Üí Add Server. Enter your WebDAV URL, username, and password.

2

Scan Your Library

Tap the refresh button to scan your server. The app will automatically detect manga, comics, and novels.

3

Start Reading

Tap any item to start reading. Swipe left/right to navigate pages. Your progress is saved automatically.

FAQ

What is WebDAV?

WebDAV is a protocol that lets apps access files on a remote server. Most NAS devices (Synology, QNAP) support WebDAV.

Why are some images not loading?

Check your network connection and ensure the server is accessible. Try refreshing the library.

How do I restore my Pro license?

Go to Settings ‚Üí About ‚Üí Upgrade, then enter your license key to restore your purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription through Settings ‚Üí Apple ID ‚Üí Subscriptions on your device.
